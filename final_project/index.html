What is Elden Builds?

Elden Builds is a comprehensive tool designed to help you plan, create, and optimize your character builds for Elden Ring. Whether you're a seasoned Tarnished or just beginning your journey in the Lands Between, our platform provides all the tools you need to experiment with different builds before committing your precious runes.

How It Works

1. Explore Items

Browse our comprehensive database of weapons, armor, talismans, sorceries, and incantations. View detailed stats, requirements, and effects for every item in the game.

2. Create Your Build

Use our character sheet interface to select weapons, armor pieces, talismans, and spells. See real-time stat calculations and equipment requirements as you build.

3. Save & Compare

Save multiple builds and switch between them instantly. Compare different loadouts to find the perfect combination for your playstyle.

4. Optimize

View total weight, damage output, defense stats, and attribute requirements. Ensure your build is optimized before you invest in-game.
