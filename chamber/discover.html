Antelope Island State Park

4528 W 1700 S, Syracuse, UT 84075

A stunning natural wonder featuring free-roaming bison and antelope herds, scenic hiking trails, and breathtaking views of the Great Salt Lake. Perfect for wildlife viewing and outdoor adventures.

Hill Aerospace Museum

7961 Wardleigh Rd, Hill AFB, UT 84056

Explore an impressive collection of historic aircraft and military artifacts. This free museum showcases over 90 aircraft and missiles, offering a fascinating journey through aviation history.

Great Salt Lake Marina

13312 W Frontage Rd, Syracuse, UT 84075

A premier waterfront destination for boating, sailing, and water recreation. Enjoy spectacular sunsets and unique perspectives of the Great Salt Lake's famous pink waters.

Layton Hills Mall

1201 N Hill Field Rd, Layton, UT 84041

The premier shopping destination in Davis County, featuring over 100 stores, diverse dining options, and entertainment venues. A perfect blend of retail therapy and family fun.

Rush Funplex

2341 S 1200 W, Syracuse, UT 84075

Northern Utah's ultimate family entertainment center with arcade games, go-karts, laser tag, mini golf, and bowling. Create unforgettable memories with friends and family.

Farmington Bay Waterfowl Management Area

Glover Ln, Farmington, UT 84025

A paradise for bird watchers and nature photographers, featuring diverse wetland habitats. Experience thousands of migratory birds and stunning natural scenery year-round.

Great Salt Lake Shorelands Preserve

1485 W 3000 S, Syracuse, UT 84075

A pristine natural preserve protecting critical wetland habitat along the Great Salt Lake shoreline. Discover unique ecosystems and enjoy peaceful nature walks.

Snowbasin Resort

World-class ski resort offering exceptional powder skiing, scenic mountain views, and year-round outdoor recreation. Experience the thrill of Olympic-quality slopes and trails.
